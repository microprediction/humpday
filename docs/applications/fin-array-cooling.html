🌡️ Heat-Sink Fin Array

A heat sink has eight cooling fins; we choose each fin's height. A taller fin has more area but its tip runs less effectively, and taller fins together choke the shared airflow so every fin cools a little less. Fins nearer the front see more airflow than those behind. The optimum makes the front fins taller and the rear ones shorter rather than sizing them all alike. Score is negative heat dissipated, to minimise. 8-D.
