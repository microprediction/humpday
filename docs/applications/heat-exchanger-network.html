🔥 Heat-Exchanger Network

A cold process stream is heated in series by six exchangers, each against a hotter stream. We choose each exchanger's area. Effectiveness rises with area but with diminishing returns, so the outlet temperature builds up stage by stage toward the target. Area costs money and the target carries a shortfall penalty, so the good design puts area where the temperature driving force is largest rather than spreading it evenly. Score is area cost plus shortfall penalty, to minimise. 6-D problem.
